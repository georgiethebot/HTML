Our Courses

Film Production 101

Introduction to the fundamentals of filmmaking. This course covers scriptwriting, directing, cinematography, editing, and sound design.

Advanced Directing Techniques

Dive deeper into the art of directing and visual storytelling. Learn advanced techniques for character development, scene composition, and narrative pacing.

Duration: 8 weeks

Screenwriting Masterclass

Learn the essentials of crafting compelling screenplays. This course covers story structure, character development, dialogue writing, and industry formatting standards.

Duration: 10 weeks

Cinematography Workshop

Hands-on training in camera operation, lighting, and composition. Explore the technical and artistic aspects of cinematography through practical exercises and projects.

Duration: 6 weeks
